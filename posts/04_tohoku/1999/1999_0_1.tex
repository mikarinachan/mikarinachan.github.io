　3つの角$\alpha$，$\beta$，$\gamma$ $(-{90}^\circ<\alpha, \, \beta, \, \gamma<{90}^\circ)$が
\[ \tan\alpha+\tan\beta+\tan\gamma=\tan\alpha\tan\beta\tan\gamma \]
をみたすとき，$\alpha+\beta+\gamma$の値をすべて求めよ．
