　楕円$\displaystyle\frac{x^2}{4}+\frac{y^2}{9}=1$上に
点$P_k(k=1, \, 2, \, \cdots, \, n)$を$\displaystyle\angle P_kOA=\frac{k}{n}\pi$を満たすようにとる．

\[ \lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{1}{n}(\frac{1}{{OP_1}^2}+\frac{1}{{OP_2}^2}+\cdots+\frac{1}{{OP_n}^2}) \]
を求めよ．
