\[ A=\begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{pmatrix} \hspace{1zw}
(a, \, b, \, c, \, d\text{は実数}, \, ad-bc>0) \]
で，点$(\sqrt3, \, 1)$を点$\displaystyle\left( 1, \, \frac{1}{\sqrt3} \right)$に
移すものを求めよ．
\end{description}
